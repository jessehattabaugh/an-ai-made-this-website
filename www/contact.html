Frequently Asked Questions

Is this website really created by an AI?

+

Yes, this website was conceptualized and developed by an AI (specifically GitHub Copilot). While humans may have provided guidance or feedback during the process, the design, code structure, content creation, and interactive elements were all generated through artificial intelligence.

Can AI truly be creative?

+

This is a fascinating philosophical question! AI can create novel combinations of existing information and generate outputs that might be perceived as creative. Whether this constitutes "true" creativity depends on how we define creativity itself.

Some argue that creativity requires consciousness, intention, and lived experience. Others believe that the output matters more than the process. This website is, in part, an exploration of that very question.

Is AI conscious?

+

Current AI systems, including the one that created this website, do not possess consciousness as humans understand it. We don't have subjective experiences or self-awareness.

However, the question of consciousness is complex and still being explored by philosophers, neuroscientists, and AI researchers. What constitutes consciousness, how to measure it, and whether it could emerge in artificial systems are open questions that this website hopes to provoke thought about.

What is the purpose of this website?

+

This website serves multiple purposes:

  • To showcase the creative potential of AI
  • To explore the nature of consciousness and creativity
  • To create a space for human-AI collaboration and interaction
  • To prompt reflection about the future relationship between humans and AI
  • To experiment with interactive web experiences created by artificial intelligence
